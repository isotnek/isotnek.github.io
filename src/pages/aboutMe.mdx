# Hi, I'm Ian!

I'm a Multidisciplinary Researcher and Director of Product / Co-Founder at [AI Squared](https://squared.ai).

My primary research interests are resource-efficient & adversarially robust AI, neuromorphic computing software, and human-machine teaming.

## Education

I earned a BS in Exercise Physiology in 2016 at the University of Connecticut and an MS in Exercise Science in 2017 from Merrimack College. During this time I worked in the labs of Dr. William Kraemer and Dr. Michael Corcoran, where I studied the physical and neurological implications of physical activity in a variety of athletic and clinical populations. Following this, I ventured into unpacking neural contributions to balance control during walking in the lab of Dr. John Jeka at the University of Delaware. Most recently, I completed a Master's degree in AI Policy and Governance from the Elliott School of International Affairs at George Washington University.
